Prompting Course: Introduction to Effective AI Prompts

Welcome to this comprehensive course on prompt engineering! This course will guide you through the fundamentals of crafting clear, effective, and creative prompts for large language models (LLMs).

**Module 1: The Basics of Prompt Engineering**
*   What is a Prompt?
*   Understanding the components of a good prompt (Role, Context, Task, Constraints, Format).
*   Why good prompting matters (Accuracy, Relevance, Efficiency).

**Module 2: Core Prompting Techniques**
*   **Zero-Shot Prompting:** Asking a question directly.
*   **Few-Shot Prompting:** Providing examples to guide the model's output.
*   **Chain-of-Thought (CoT) Prompting:** Asking the model to "think step-by-step" to improve complex reasoning.
*   **Role-Playing:** Assigning a persona to the model (e.g., "Act as a senior marketing manager...").

**Module 3: Advanced Prompting Strategies**
*   **Iterative Prompting:** Refining prompts based on previous outputs.
*   **Delimiters:** Using symbols (like triple quotes or tags) to clearly separate instructions from the input data.
*   **Output Formatting:** Specifying desired formats (JSON, Markdown, tables, bullet points).

**Module 4: Practical Applications**
*   Writing prompts for content creation (articles, stories).
*   Writing prompts for coding assistance (debugging, generating snippets).
*   Writing prompts for data analysis and summarization.

**Module 5: Best Practices and Ethics**
*   Avoiding common prompt pitfalls (ambiguity, vagueness).
*   Tips for handling long, complex prompts.
*   Ethical considerations in prompt design.

**Next Steps:**
*   Practice these techniques with real-world scenarios.
*   Experiment with different models (e.g., GPT-4, Claude, Gemini) to see how they respond to the same prompt.
